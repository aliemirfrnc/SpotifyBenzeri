## Slide 2: The Problem
**Language learning is broken.**
- **High Churn:** 80% of users abandon language apps within 3 months.
- **Boring Context:** Users are forced to learn artificial sentences ("The apple is red") rather than culturally relevant material.
- **Expensive Practice:** Practicing real pronunciation with native tutors costs upwards of $30/hour, excluding 95% of the market.

## Slide 3: The Secret to Habit Building

People already have an unbreakable daily habit: **Streaming Music.**
*Gen Z and Millennials spend an average of 2.5 hours a day on Spotify.*

## Slide 6: How It Works - Active Shadowing
*Screenshot showing Shadowing Mode.*
We don't just teach reading. We teach speaking.
**Shadowing Mode:** Users sing along to the track. Lingofy's Pronunciation Coach (Whisper AI) listens, analyzes phonemes, and scores their fluency, rhythm, and stress.

---

## Slide 7: AI Infrastructure (Our Tech Moat)
We are not a ChatGPT wrapper.
- **Factory Routed AI:** Dynamic switching between Groq (Llama-3) for zero-latency translations and OpenRouter for deep grammar analysis.
- **24-Hour TTL Caching:** For top Billboard hits, API costs are $0. Our infrastructure scales logarithmically, not linearly.
